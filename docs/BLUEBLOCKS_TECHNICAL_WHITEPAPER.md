- **ZK-Proofs** for privacy-preserving verification
- **Supply Chain** tracking for medicine and food safety
- **Smart Contracts** for automated insurance claims

We create a unified platform that replaces fragmented, inefficient legacy systems with a modern, secure, patient-centric ecosystem.
