## ARTICLE 3: SUBCONTRACTOR OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 Permitted Uses**

Subcontractor shall:
- (a) Use PHI only as necessary to provide contracted services;
- (b) Not use PHI for marketing without authorization;
- (c) Not sell PHI under any circumstances;
- (d) Apply Minimum Necessary standard.

**3.2 Security Requirements**

Subcontractor shall implement:

| Control | Requirement | Evidence Required |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Encryption at Rest | AES-256 minimum | SOC 2 report |
| Encryption in Transit | TLS 1.2+ | Certificate |
| Access Control | MFA, RBAC | Policy document |
| Audit Logging | 6-year retention | System config |
| Incident Response | 24-hour notification | IR plan |
| Employee Training | Annual HIPAA training | Training records |

**3.3 Breach Notification**

- (a) Report suspected breaches within 24 hours;
- (b) Provide detailed breach report within 72 hours;
- (c) Cooperate fully in investigation;
- (d) Bear notification costs for breaches caused by Subcontractor.

**3.4 Downstream Subcontractors**

- (a) Written authorization required for further subcontracting;
- (b) Equivalent BAA required with all downstream parties;
- (c) Subcontractor remains liable for downstream performance.

**3.5 Documentation**

Subcontractor shall maintain and provide upon request:
- (a) Security policies and procedures;
- (b) Risk assessment documentation;
- (c) Training records;
- (d) Incident response history;
- (e) Audit reports.
