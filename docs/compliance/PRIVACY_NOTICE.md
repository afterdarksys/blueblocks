## 1. Who We Are

BlueBlocks, Inc. ("BlueBlocks," "we," "us," or "our") operates a healthcare blockchain platform designed to securely store, transmit, and manage health information. We serve as a Business Associate to healthcare providers, health plans, and other Covered Entities under HIPAA.

---

## 2. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We understand the importance of protecting your health information. This notice explains:

- How we collect and use your health information
- When we may share your health information
- Your rights regarding your health information
- Our legal duties

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Health Information

We may receive and process:
- **Demographic Information**: Name, address, date of birth, contact information
- **Medical Identifiers**: Medical record numbers, health plan IDs
- **Health Records**: Medical history, diagnoses, treatments, test results
- **Insurance Information**: Health plan details, claims information
- **Consent Records**: Your preferences for how information is shared

### 3.2 Technical Information

- Cryptographic hashes of health records
- Access and audit logs
- Device and session information

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 With Your Authorization

We will obtain your written authorization before:
- Sharing your information for marketing purposes
- Selling your health information
- Sharing psychotherapy notes
- Using your information for purposes not described in this notice

### 4.2 Without Your Authorization

We may use and share your information without authorization for:

**Treatment**: Sharing information with healthcare providers involved in your care

**Payment**: Processing insurance claims and verifying coverage

**Healthcare Operations**: Quality improvement, auditing, and compliance activities

**Required by Law**: Responding to court orders, subpoenas, or legal mandates

**Public Health**: Reporting diseases, injuries, and vital statistics as required

**Health Oversight**: Audits and investigations by government agencies

**Judicial Proceedings**: Court orders and legal proceedings

**Law Enforcement**: Certain limited circumstances required by law

**Emergencies**: When necessary to prevent serious harm

**Research**: Studies approved by privacy boards (with safeguards)

**Organ Donation**: Facilitating organ and tissue donation

**Workers' Compensation**: Claims related to workplace injuries

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 Right to Access

You have the right to see and get a copy of your health information. To request access:
- Submit a written request to our Privacy Officer
- We will respond within 30 days (may extend to 60 days with notice)
- We may charge a reasonable fee for copies

### 5.2 Right to Amend

If you believe your information is incorrect or incomplete:
- Submit a written request explaining the desired amendment
- We will respond within 60 days
- We may deny the request under certain circumstances

### 5.3 Right to an Accounting of Disclosures

You may request a list of disclosures we have made:
- Covers disclosures in the prior 6 years
- Excludes disclosures for treatment, payment, or operations
- First request in any 12-month period is free

### 5.4 Right to Request Restrictions

You may request restrictions on how we use or share your information:
- We are not required to agree to all restrictions
- We must agree to restrict disclosures to health plans for services you pay for in full

### 5.5 Right to Confidential Communications

You may request that we communicate with you:
- At a specific address or phone number
- By a specific method

### 5.6 Right to a Paper Copy

You may request a paper copy of this notice at any time.

### 5.7 Right to File a Complaint

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated:
- File a complaint with our Privacy Officer
- File a complaint with the HHS Office for Civil Rights
- We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint

## 6. Our Duties

We are required to:
- Maintain the privacy of your health information
- Provide you with this notice of our privacy practices
- Follow the terms of this notice
- Notify you if there is a breach of your unsecured health information

## 7. Blockchain-Specific Privacy Protections

### 7.1 Encryption

All health information stored on the BlueBlocks blockchain is:
- Encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
- Accessible only with your encryption keys
- Protected by cryptographic access controls

### 7.2 Patient-Controlled Keys

- You control the encryption keys to your health information
- Without your keys, your data cannot be decrypted
- You decide who can access your information

### 7.3 Immutable Audit Trail

- All access to your information is logged
- Logs cannot be altered or deleted
- You can review who has accessed your information

### 7.4 Data Minimization

- We store only cryptographic hashes on the public blockchain
- Full health records are stored in encrypted off-chain storage
- We collect only information necessary for services
